YouTube downloader

Easy to use. Open source. Ad-free.

Don't select Video [best quality] unless you know that your video player can play MKV and WebM files (and Opus audio).

Don't select Audio [best] unless you know that you're able to play Opus (.opus) files as that's usually the format of Audio [best].

If using an iOS device, select Video [MP4] or Audio [MP3] as iOS does not support all media formats natively.

Paste YouTube link (URL):



Audio [best] usually gives you a file with a bitrate of ~160 kbps. This does not mean that the audio has lower quality than the MP3 option. The best quality audio stream in a YouTube video is usually ~160 kbps Opus. YouTube downloaders that give you a higher bitrate file are doing lossy-to-lossy conversion, giving you lower audio quality than the Audio [best] option on this website.
